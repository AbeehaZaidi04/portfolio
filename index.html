About me

Introduction
Abeeha Zaidi · MSU

I'm Abeeha — a supply chain planner at Microsoft by title, but a researcher at heart. Born in Pakistan, raised in Dubai, and in the U.S. since 2017, I've spent most of my life watching the same products behave differently in different markets — and that's the curiosity that pulled me into research.

I studied Supply Chain at Michigan State's Broad College, graduated in 2025, and interned at Toyota, Intel, and Nike along the way. I founded ISCRO — the Interdisciplinary Supply Chain Research Organization — to give students a real sandbox for economic and market analysis, and I write about global trade & supply strategy on Substack and a personal blog.

Outside the spreadsheet: tennis, soccer, long runs, and whatever I'm reading at the moment (currently My Feudal Lord by Tehmina Durrani).

Project № 01 · Featured

Windows PC Demand Outlook

Independent Market Research Seattle, WA · 2026 Solo Analyst · 4-week effort
Market research Demand forecasting Scenario modeling Secondary data
Research question
How will demand for Windows PCs evolve globally over the next 18–24 months (Q2 2026 through Q4 2027), and what does that imply for Windows commercial planning assumptions and Copilot+ PC go-to-market investment sizing?
Process
Seven-phase methodology executed over a 4-week solo effort using secondary data only. Phase 1 established research scope with fictional-but-realistic stakeholder framing. Phase 2 developed hypotheses against analyst consensus. Phase 3 built data strategy, pressure-tested the contrarian attack using 2008–2010 and 2021–2023 historical analogs, and decomposed 2025 growth into three independent drivers. Phase 4 structured findings into four analytical lenses. Phase 5 built quantified scenarios with probability weights. Phase 6 synthesized five ranked insights with calibrated confidence levels. Phase 7 consolidated deliverables.
Data sources
Analyst firms, primary company filings, government & macro data, and industry press.

Project № 02

Spectrum Consulting: Hospitality industry analysis.

Spectrum Consulting Group East Lansing, MI · 2023 Director of Finance · PM
Industry research White paper Hospitality
Brief
Understand how post-pandemic consumer behavior, labor shortages, and shifting distribution channels were reshaping the hospitality sector — and translate that into a client-facing point of view the consulting group could use in new-business conversations.
Strategy
Segmented the industry by business model (full-service, limited-service, experiential), synthesized economic and trade data on labor and occupancy, and interviewed operators to pressure-test the secondary research. Framed findings around three tensions: cost structure, loyalty erosion, and channel control.
Impact
Produced a published white paper that became Spectrum's anchor asset in hospitality-sector pitches, informed two client engagements, and helped the group diversify beyond its traditional tech-and-retail pipeline.

Project № 04

CROX: a financial analysis & valuation.

Independent research 2024 Financial analyst
DCF Equity research Consumer
Brief
Evaluate Crocs, Inc. (CROX) as a long-term equity — does the brand's momentum hold up once HEYDUDE stabilizes, and is the market mispricing its earnings durability?

Project № 05

Starting ISCRO: understanding the student-researcher market.

Michigan State University East Lansing, MI · 2023 — 25 Founder & President
Market sizing Student org Go-to-market
Brief
No student org at MSU offered supply-chain students a way to produce original research — only case prep and recruiting. Size that gap and decide whether a new org could realistically sustain itself.
Strategy
Interviewed ~30 students across Broad to map what they actually wanted (research, writing, data skills vs. more consulting case prep). Positioned ISCRO as complementary — not competitive — to existing orgs, with a low-commitment entry point and a publishing track that doubled as portfolio material for members.
Impact
Grew to 15+ active student researchers and published multiple pieces (container market, Red Sea crisis, digital marketing). The org still operates post-graduation.
